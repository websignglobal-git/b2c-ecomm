Process That Works

Following Agile methodology, we tackle problems efficiently and collaboratively.
01

Define

What are your goals?

Accounting for your goals, resources, timelines and technologies, we'll work with you to plan creative and effective solutions that work for everyone involved. Then we document everything to ensure we're aligned.

02

Build

How will we achieve your goals?

After writing down the planned solution and shared idea of success, we jump into action. We'll check in at key milestones and regular intervals to make sure you always know what's going on with your projects.

03

Analyze

How did our work perform?

After launching an initiative, we zoom out and look for the next opportunity. Is the initiative accomplishing the goals we laid out in the definition step? Should we optimize it further, or is there a better opportunity we can invest in?

04

Iterate

Repeat.?

Following our tried and true iterative development process, we'll continue to collaboratively improve your digital eCommerce experience by defining, planning, and analyzing new initiatives.
